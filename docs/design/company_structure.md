# CodeGen IT Solutions: Company Structure

## Company Vision

To provide autonomous software engineering solutions to small and medium-sized businesses, leveraging AI agents to accelerate development and improve code quality.

## Company Culture

* **Innovation-driven**: Inspired by Apple's unitary organizational form, the company will foster a culture of innovation where all departments work together on each project.
* **Flat and Agile**: Inspired by the flat structure of startups and Amazon's "two-pizza rule", the company will have a relatively flat hierarchy with small, autonomous teams to encourage creativity and productivity.
* **Remote-first**: The company will be remote-first, hiring talent from anywhere in the world.
* **Open Source Contribution**: The company will actively contribute to open-source projects, following the example of "Codegen".

## Company Structure

The company will have a hybrid structure, combining elements of a functional and a matrix structure. It will be organized into the following departments:

### 1. Executive Leadership

* **CEO (Chief Executive Officer)**: Responsible for the overall vision, strategy, and direction of the company.
* **CTO (Chief Technology Officer)**: Responsible for the technology strategy, product development, and innovation.

### 2. Engineering Department

This will be the core of the company, responsible for developing and maintaining the AI agents and the platform.

* **Head of Engineering**: Leads the engineering department.
* **AI/ML Engineering Team**:
  * AI/ML Engineers: Responsible for developing and training the AI models for the agents.
  * Data Scientists: Responsible for analyzing data and improving the performance of the agents.
* **Platform Engineering Team**:
  * Backend Engineers: Responsible for developing and maintaining the backend of the platform.
  * Frontend Engineers: Responsible for developing and maintaining the user interface of the platform.
* **DevOps Team**:
  * DevOps Engineers: Responsible for the infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, and deployment of the platform.
* **Quality Assurance (QA) Team**:
  * QA Engineers: Responsible for testing the platform and the agents.

### 3. Go-To-Market (GTM) Department

This department will be responsible for sales, marketing, and customer success.

* **Head of GTM**: Leads the GTM department.
* **Sales Team**:
  * Account Executives: Responsible for acquiring new customers.
* **Marketing Team**:
  * Content Creators: Responsible for creating marketing content (blog posts, articles, etc.).
  * Digital Marketers: Responsible for online marketing campaigns.
* **Customer Success Team**:
  * Customer Success Managers: Responsible for onboarding new customers and ensuring their success.
  * Support Engineers: Responsible for providing technical support to customers.

### 4. General & Administration Department

* **Head of Operations**: Responsible for the day-to-day operations of the company.
* **HR Manager**: Responsible for hiring, onboarding, and employee relations.
* **Finance Manager**: Responsible for the company's finances.

## Project Teams

For each project, a cross-functional team will be formed, consisting of members from different departments. This is the "matrix" part of the structure. Each project team will have a designated "Project Lead" who is responsible for the successful delivery of the project. The Project Lead will report to the CTO.

This structure is designed to be flexible and scalable. As the company grows, new teams can be added to each department, and new project teams can be formed as needed.
